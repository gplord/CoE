Doug Reside, Ph.D.

Doug Reside became the first Digital Curator of the Performing Arts at New York Public Library in February of 2011 after serving for four and a half years on the directorial staff of the Maryland Institute for Technology in the Humanities (MITH) at the University of Maryland in College Park. He has led numerous Digital Humanities projects and is currently editing the Musical of the Month blog at NYPL which makes available one musical theater libretto each month in various ebook formats.

dougreside@gmail.com

Gregory Lord

Gregory Lord is the Lead Designer & Software Engineer at the Digital Humanities Initiative (DHi) at Hamilton College. Prior to his time at DHi, Greg served for six years as a Web Designer and Web Developer at the Maryland Institute for Technology in the Humanities. Greg has developed logo, web, graphic, and interface designs, as well as developing web technologies for countless projects over the years, and in 2010 his experience led him to found ZenGrove Design, LLC, a small webdesign & development company based in Maryland.

gplord@gmail.com
